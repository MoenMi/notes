# Formulas

## Discrete Distributions

### Discrete Uniform (1, N)

$$
p_X(x) =
\begin{cases}
    \frac{1}{N} & \text{if } x = 1, 2, ..., N \\
    0 & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

$$ E(X) = \frac{N+1}{2} $$

$$ Var(X) = \frac{N^2-1}{12} $$
